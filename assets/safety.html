Some words about safety of your Bitcoins:

  • Bitcoins in Bitcoin Wallet are stored on the device. If you lose your device, you'll lose your Bitcoins. Just like money in your physical wallet.
  • Before uninstalling Bitcoin Wallet (or wiping your device), transfer your Bitcoins to another wallet. Any Bitcoins remaining will be lost.
  • Before upgrading Bitcoin Wallet, always consult the release notes in Android Market first! Never activate auto-upgrade.
  • Transactions cannot be undone. If you send your Bitcoins into the void, there is no way to get them back unless you know the receiver and he/she is willing to send them back.
  • Keep your Android device safe! Do only install apps you fully trust. Malicious apps could be trying to steal your wallet.
  • Keep the risk low! Only use Android Wallet with small amounts for day use, and keep the rest of your Bitcoins at a safer place.
  • If you just want to try Bitcoin without risk (but without the ability to actually pay for something), consider using Bitcoin Wallet for Testnet.
