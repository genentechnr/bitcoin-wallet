The upper left part of the screen displays your bitcoin address that other people need to know in order to send you bitcoins. Tap on the address for pasting to clipboard. Long tap to share your address via short message, mail, tweet and the like.

The QR code on the right contains your bitcoin address and can be used for scanning by another party. If the scanning device runs Android, a QR code scanning app like Goggles is needed. Tap on the QR code to get a larger version of it.
